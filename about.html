About Page

This Study Tracker is designed to help you efficiently track how many hours you truly study.

This is especially useful as we often overestimate how long, we work/study for. According to this study we can overestimate our working hours by as much as 50%!

To combat this I have created this Study Hour Logger, where, whilst you study you can log your true hours worked. This will return a more accurate depiction of your workrate.

As a result you can take the necessary steps to improve it if it is lacking.

It is recommended that in order to be as efficient as possible, you study in no more than 4-hour blocks. This is because of the concentration limitations of your brain. So once you hit those 4 hours go take a break and start another block!

In future, we plan to add proper timers to the site or even perhaps, create a Google Extension.
